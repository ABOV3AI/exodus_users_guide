# Configuring Exodus with your Claude Pro/Enterprise tokens

Subscribers to Claude Pro and Claude Enterprise can utilize the Claude models within the ABOV3 Exodus environment.

1) You need to be logged into Claude Pro/Enterprise in your browser.
2) At the top of the Exodus screen, where it displays your current model, pull that down and at the bottom where it says "Models" click there.

<img src="./images/Screenshot 2026-03-28 064545.png" style="width: 350px; height: 500px;">   

3) Click the "Login with Claude Pro/Max" button to begin the process.  Incidentally, if you have a regular Claude API account, this is where you would enter that key as well.

<img src="./images/Screenshot 2026-03-28 065733.png">

4) A new popup broser dialog will appear asking you to authorize access to your Claude Pro/Enterprise account.  After you click Authorize it presents a JWT token (livespan seems to be about 6 hours) - copy this.

<img src="./images/Screenshot 2026-03-28 070229.png">

<img src="./images/Screenshot 2026-03-28 070419.png">

5) Paste that into the Exodus dialog and you're ready to go.

<img src="./images/Screenshot 2026-03-28 070505.png">
